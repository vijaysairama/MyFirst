WHAT WE DO

At Communicate India, we do not apply one formula for all. We listen to you, understand your situation and needs, understand your competitors - then our research experts dig deep and produce an extensive report, based on which our strategy pundits tailor-make a plan with a holistic approach to achieve what you intend to for your business.


PR

Good PR is all about the creative strategy to sell a brand. Through our integrated PR plans we have successfully launched several brands along with our strong team of individuals who are passionate about PR. The bedrock of the company, PR is in our DNA and always will be a core vertical.


Marketing

Our integrated approach to marketing is what makes us the best in the business. Rest assured our marketing team of big thinkers and go-getters will create sustainable growth for your brands. Our complete marketing services include media planning, promotions and much more.


Social Media

Social media has made the world a smaller place and helps brands connect with a larger number of users than ever before. Through our innovative social media management and content strategies we leverage your brand’s presence across all social media. As part of a larger digital marketing offering, we also provide SEO, SEM and website development.


Content Writing

Our content writing team understands that your communication is at the crux of a good brand. The content writing services offered by us include website content, branding materials etc. We go beyond traditional content writing services to include visual content, scripts and internal communications in our offerings.


Event Management

Our event management team successfully creates events for varied brands. Through event management we curate an unforgettable experience which ranks high on aesthetic appeal as well. Our effective event management strategies have successfully been implemented to conduct several high profile product launches, trade shows, auctions and more.
